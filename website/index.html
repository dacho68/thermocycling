GASOSYN Digester Technology

  • The unique Canadian Technology of Thermocycling using water, waste water, sea water in the waste treatment process (35T/h)
  • It uses low negative pressures and temperature up to 1200 C degrees
  • Combustion with energy recovery
  • Proven Technology

Biomass Byproducts of the 35T/h Unit (per day)

  • Distilled Water (840 000 liter)
  • Biodiesel (1680 barrels) or Electricity (840 MW)
  • Carbon Credit (1680*2 for biodiesel or 840 for electricity)

Social Impact and Environmentally Friendly

  • Eliminate of Waste Water
